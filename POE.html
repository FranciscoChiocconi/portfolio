Poe Projects

Alternative Energy Vehicle

November 2018, Principles of Engineering

The objective of our project was to make a car run on solar panels or hydrogen fuel cells. Our task was to make a car to drive 1 meter using this alternative energy and our group was able to accomplish it. We had the solar panels and hydrogen connected to a breadboard in series. For the the motor we connected to a 7:3 gear ratio in order to make the vehicle more efficient. We originally started with a smaller one but the gear ratio was too large. When we tested the 1 meter of the car it took 8.2 seconds and the efficiency of was 22 percent. Overall the project was a success, the car was able to move one meter in a decent time.

West-Point Bridge Project

March 2019, Principles of Engineering

The objective of our project was to make a bridge that can get the truck across the river. We had constraints in this project such as, at least 12 meters, under 300k, and much more. During this project we expiremented with the program in order to figuire out the mechanics of bridges in order to create better and cheaper bridges. The final product of our group group was a bridge that cost 248k and could maintain stability the whole time when the truck went over the bridge. We decided to choose this bridge because of it's simplicity and it's cheap price. This project was a challenge and it helped us learn more about bridges. Overall this project was a success as we were able to create a bridge that fit the constraints and effectively get the truck from one side to another.
