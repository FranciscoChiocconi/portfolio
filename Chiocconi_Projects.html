Bug Busters

August 29th - September 7, 2017, Computer Science and Engineering

The objective of our project was to create a game which would be fun to play and also challenging to create. The game is supposed to engage the player and make them enjoy playing the game. The game itself is placed in space and you play as a space ranger in a space ship. Your main objective is to eliminate the bugs similar to the game galaga. As you kill more bugs the game gets harder. The bugs start to move faster over time but you also gain upgrades as you kill more bugs. My partner and I decided we wanted to challenge ourselves but we decided that 1 week was not enough to create a game this complex. We remixed the game and we added the tutorial, the upgrades and difficulty increase. Overall this was a very fun project to make being that it was my first ever project in this field of coding. Forest Adventure

Unknown Date, Computer Science and Engineering

The objective of the text adventure we created is to entertain the user and enjoy the game we created. In our text adventure you act as a lost person in the woods and we ask a series of questions which will determine if you make it out alive. As you answer the questions, some will bring you closer to safety as some will take you to your death. When you answer the questions depending on what you answer then it will take you through a different series of questions. My partner and I were above schedule on the project so we decided to add a couple of features, like the code not breaking when not acceptable answers are typed and a couple more stages.
